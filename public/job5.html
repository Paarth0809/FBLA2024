Human Resources Manager

Job brief

We are seeking a skilled and experienced Human Resources Manager to join our team. As a Human Resources Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing all aspects of the HR department and ensuring that our company's policies and procedures are in line with legal requirements. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in HR management and a deep understanding of employment laws and regulations. You will play a crucial role in attracting, retaining, and developing top talent, as well as fostering a positive and inclusive work environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Policy Development and Compliance:
    • Develop and implement HR policies and procedures.
    • Maintain and update employee records and ensure compliance with data privacy regulations.
    • Stay updated on changes in employment laws and regulations and ensure company compliance.
  • Employee Management and Support:
    • Manage employee benefits and compensation programs.
    • Handle employee relations issues and provide guidance on conflict resolution.
    • Monitor and evaluate employee performance and provide feedback and coaching when necessary.
  • Recruitment and Training:
    • Oversee recruitment and selection processes, including job postings, interviews, and onboarding.
    • Conduct training and development programs for staff at all levels.

Requirements and Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in Human Resources or a related field.
  • Proven experience as an HR Manager or in a similar role.
  • In-depth knowledge of labor laws and regulations.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Strong problem-solving and decision-making abilities.
  • Ability to handle confidential information with discretion.
  • Ability to work well under pressure and meet deadlines.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package.
  • Opportunity to work with a dynamic and diverse team.
  • Flexible work schedule and remote work options.
  • Professional development and growth opportunities.
  • Employee wellness program and gym membership reimbursement.
