Pharmaceutical Product Manager

Job brief

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Pharmaceutical Product Manager to join our team. As a Pharmaceutical Product Manager, you will play a crucial role in developing and implementing strategies to drive the success of our pharmaceutical products. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure the successful delivery of products, from conception to commercialization.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct Market Research:
    • Analyze competitor products.
    • Identify market trends to gain insights into customer needs and preferences.
  • Develop and Execute Product Strategies:
    • Create strategies for pricing, positioning, and promotional activities.
    • Aim to maximize sales and market share.
  • Collaborate with R&D Team:
    • Work closely with the research and development team.
    • Ensure the timely development and launch of innovative pharmaceutical products.

Requirements and Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy, Life Sciences, Business, or a related field (MBA preferred).
  • Proven experience as a Product Manager in the pharmaceutical industry.
  • Strong understanding of market research and data analysis methods.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a cross-functional team environment.
  • Strong project management skills with attention to detail.
  • Knowledge of regulatory requirements and compliance in the pharmaceutical industry.
  • Proficiency in using product management software and tools.

To excel in this role, you must have a deep understanding of the pharmaceutical industry, including regulatory requirements and market dynamics. You should possess strong analytical skills to interpret market data and make informed decisions. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills are essential, as you will collaborate with various stakeholders, including sales teams, healthcare professionals, and key opinion leaders.
