Inventory Control Manager

Job brief

We are seeking an experienced and detail-oriented Inventory Control Manager to join our team. As an Inventory Control Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing and managing all aspects of our inventory control processes. You will play a crucial role in optimizing inventory levels, reducing costs, and ensuring accurate and efficient inventory management.

Key Responsibilities

  • Inventory Control and Policies:
    • Develop and implement inventory control policies and procedures to ensure accurate inventory levels and minimize stock discrepancies.
    • Implement and maintain inventory control systems, including barcode scanning and RFID technology.
    • Train and supervise inventory control staff, ensuring compliance with inventory procedures and safety regulations.
    • Update and maintain inventory records, including item descriptions, quantities, and locations.
    • Conduct regular physical inventory counts and reconcile stock discrepancies.
  • Inventory Analysis and Replenishment:
    • Monitor and analyze inventory movements and trends to forecast demand and optimize inventory replenishment.
    • Coordinate with purchasing and production departments to ensure timely and efficient inventory replenishment.
    • Develop and maintain relationships with suppliers and vendors to ensure timely and accurate delivery of inventory.
  • Collaboration and Reporting:
    • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and resolve inventory-related issues.
    • Prepare and present inventory reports to senior management, highlighting key performance indicators and recommending improvements.

Requirements and Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in business, supply chain management, or a related field.
  • Proven experience in inventory control management, preferably in a fast-paced environment.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to interpret data and make informed decisions.
  • Excellent attention to detail and organizational skills.
  • Proficiency in inventory control software and Microsoft Office Suite.
  • Knowledge of supply chain management principles and best practices.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to work effectively in a team.
  • Ability to prioritize tasks and meet tight deadlines.
  • Familiarity with warehouse management systems is a plus.
  • Ability to adapt to changing priorities and work well under pressure.
  • Strong leadership skills and the ability to motivate and inspire a team.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary package.
  • Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
  • Retirement savings plan with company matching.
  • Paid time off and flexible work schedule.
  • Opportunities for professional development and advancement.
