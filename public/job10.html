Clinical Data Manager

Job brief

We are currently seeking a skilled Clinical Data Manager to join our team. As a Clinical Data Manager, you will play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and efficient collection, storage, and analysis of clinical trial data. You will be responsible for managing and organizing large quantities of clinical data, ensuring its integrity and quality, and providing support to the clinical research team.

Key Responsibilities

  • Clinical Data Management Oversight:
    • Oversee all aspects of clinical data management activities, including data collection, storage, and analysis.
    • Develop and implement data management plans for clinical trials, ensuring compliance with relevant regulations and guidelines.
    • Create and maintain data management tools, including electronic data capture (EDC) systems and databases.
  • Data Quality and Standards:
    • Ensure data accuracy, completeness, and quality by conducting regular quality checks and resolving data discrepancies.
    • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to establish data standards and ensure consistency across clinical trials.
  • Data Reporting and Coordination:
    • Coordinate data transfers and exchanges with external vendors, ensuring data security and confidentiality.
    • Generate and present data reports to stakeholders, including study investigators and regulatory authorities.

Requirements and Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in a relevant field, such as life sciences, statistics, or informatics.
  • At least 3 years of experience in clinical data management, preferably in a pharmaceutical or biotech setting.
  • Strong knowledge of clinical data management principles, including CDISC standards and regulatory requirements.
  • Proficiency in using various clinical data management tools and software, such as EDC systems and SAS programming.
  • Excellent attention to detail and organizational skills, with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills, with the ability to identify and resolve data-related issues.
  • Effective communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams and external stakeholders.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
  • Flexible work schedule and remote work options.
  • Collaborative and supportive work environment.
  • Access to cutting-edge technology and resources.
