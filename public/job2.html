Pharmaceutical Sales Representative

Job brief

We are seeking a highly motivated and results-driven Pharmaceutical Sales Representative to join our team. In this role, you will be responsible for promoting and selling pharmaceutical products to healthcare professionals, including doctors, pharmacists, and hospitals. This is an exciting opportunity to make a real impact in the healthcare industry and contribute to the success of our organization. As a Pharmaceutical Sales Representative, you will build strong relationships with healthcare professionals and provide them with up-to-date information on our products. You will be responsible for developing and implementing strategic sales plans to achieve sales targets and increase market share.

Key Responsibilities

  • Sales and Promotion:
    • Promote and sell pharmaceutical products to healthcare professionals.
    • Provide up-to-date information on product features and benefits to healthcare professionals.
    • Develop and implement strategic sales plans to achieve sales targets.
    • Conduct product presentations to healthcare professionals.
  • Relationship Management:
    • Develop and maintain relationships with doctors, pharmacists, and hospitals.
    • Provide feedback on market trends and competitor activity.
  • Market Intelligence:
    • Attend medical conferences and trade shows to promote products and gather market intelligence.

Requirements and Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in a relevant field.
  • Proven track record in pharmaceutical sales.
  • Strong understanding of the healthcare industry and regulations.
  • Excellent communication and negotiation skills.
  • Ability to build and maintain relationships with healthcare professionals.
  • Self-motivated and able to work independently.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills.
  • Valid driver's license and ability to travel as required by the role.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and bonus structure.
  • Comprehensive health and dental insurance packages.
  • Generous vacation and paid time off policy.
  • Continuous professional development and advancement opportunities.
  • Company car and expense account for travel and client visits.

Additionally, you will be involved in organizing and conducting product presentations, attending medical conferences and trade shows, and providing feedback on market trends and competitor activity. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record in pharmaceutical sales, with a strong understanding of the healthcare industry and a network of contacts in the field. Excellent communication and negotiation skills are essential, as well as the ability to work independently and as part of a team. This role requires a high level of self-motivation and the ability to effectively manage your time and prioritize tasks.
