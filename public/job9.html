Database Administrator

Job brief

We are seeking a skilled and experienced Database Administrator to join our team. As a Database Administrator, you will be responsible for maintaining, optimizing, and securing our company's databases. You will work closely with our IT team to ensure that our databases are running efficiently and effectively to support our business operations. In this role, you will have the opportunity to make a significant impact by ensuring the reliability and performance of our databases. You will handle tasks such as database design, performance tuning, data backups, and troubleshooting any database-related issues. As a Database Administrator, you will also be responsible for implementing and maintaining security measures to safeguard our data.

Key Responsibilities

  • Database Design and Development:
    • Designing, developing, and maintaining relational databases.
    • Collaborating with software developers to design database solutions.
  • Database Maintenance and Security:
    • Ensuring the integrity and consistency of data.
    • Managing database security, including user access control and data encryption.
    • Conducting regular backups and data recovery operations.
  • Performance and Troubleshooting:
    • Optimizing database performance and tuning queries for efficiency.
    • Troubleshooting and resolving any database-related issues.

Requirements and Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Proven work experience as a Database Administrator.
  • Proficiency in database management systems, such as Oracle, SQL Server, or MySQL.
  • In-depth knowledge of database design and implementation.
  • Strong understanding of database performance optimization techniques.
  • Experience with database security and data encryption.
  • Familiarity with backup and recovery procedures.
  • Excellent problem-solving and troubleshooting skills.
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Effective communication and collaboration skills.
  • Ability to handle confidential and sensitive information securely.
  • Relevant certifications, such as Oracle Certified Professional (OCP) or Microsoft Certified Database Administrator (MCDBA), are a plus.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and compensation package.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance plans.
  • Generous vacation and paid time off policy.
  • Retirement savings plan with company match.
  • Opportunities for career growth and professional development.
