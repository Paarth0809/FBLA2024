Quality Assurance Manager

Job brief

We are looking for a highly skilled and experienced Quality Assurance Manager to join our team. As a Quality Assurance Manager, you will be responsible for ensuring that our products and services meet the highest quality standards and customer expectations. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify areas for improvement and implement processes to enhance quality control and assurance.

Key Responsibilities

  • Policy Development and Compliance:
    • Develop and implement quality assurance policies and procedures to ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations.
    • Oversee the documentation and maintenance of quality assurance records, including inspection and test reports, quality audits, and non-conformance reports.
  • Quality Monitoring and Improvement:
    • Monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of quality assurance processes and make recommendations for improvement.
    • Conduct regular audits of processes, systems, and products to identify areas for improvement and potential risks.
    • Track and analyze quality metrics to identify trends and develop strategies to improve overall product and service quality.
  • Training and Collaboration:
    • Train and educate staff on quality assurance principles and practices to promote a culture of quality excellence.
    • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement corrective and preventive actions to address quality issues.

Requirements and Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in a relevant field such as quality assurance, industrial engineering, or a related discipline.
  • Proven experience as a Quality Assurance Manager or a similar role, with a strong track record of implementing and managing quality systems and processes.
  • In-depth knowledge of quality management principles, practices, and tools, including ISO and other relevant quality standards.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to identify root causes of quality issues and develop effective solutions.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams and senior management.
  • Detail-oriented and organized, with a strong focus on accuracy and attention to detail.
  • Strong leadership and people management skills, with the ability to motivate and inspire a team to achieve quality objectives.
  • Proactive and self-motivated, with the ability to work independently and manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and bonus opportunities.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance plans.
  • Generous paid time off, including vacation and sick days.
  • 401(k) retirement savings plan with company match.
  • Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
