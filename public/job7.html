Supply Chain Manager

Job brief

As a Supply Chain Manager, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and effectiveness of our supply chain operations. You will be responsible for overseeing the entire supply chain process, from procurement to delivery, and implementing strategies to optimize inventory levels, reduce costs, and enhance overall productivity. Your exceptional analytical skills, strong problem-solving abilities, and proven track record in supply chain management will be invaluable in driving continuous improvement and achieving operational excellence.

Key Responsibilities

  • Supply Chain Planning and Strategy:
    • Plan and execute end-to-end supply chain strategies in line with company goals and objectives.
    • Monitor and forecast demand to ensure sufficient inventory availability while minimizing excess stock.
    • Analyze and evaluate supply chain data to identify areas for improvement and implement process enhancements.
  • Supplier and Inventory Management:
    • Coordinate with suppliers and negotiate favorable terms and conditions to achieve cost savings and mitigate risks.
    • Develop and maintain inventory management systems and protocols to optimize stock levels and minimize obsolescence.
    • Manage relationships with key stakeholders, including vendors, manufacturers, and distributors, to ensure timely and accurate delivery of products.
  • Collaboration and Performance Tracking:
    • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, such as sales, marketing, and production, to align supply chain operations with business strategies.
    • Track and report key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure supply chain performance and drive operational efficiency.

Requirements and Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in supply chain management, logistics, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a Supply Chain Manager or a similar role.
  • In-depth knowledge of supply chain principles, practices, and procedures.
  • Strong analytical skills and ability to use data-driven insights to make informed decisions.
  • Proficiency in supply chain software and tools such as SAP, Oracle, or JDA.
  • Excellent communication and negotiation skills with the ability to build and maintain relationships with stakeholders at all levels.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and effectively manage multiple priorities.
  • Strong leadership and team management abilities to drive performance and foster a collaborative work culture.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunity for career growth and development.
  • Work-life balance with flexible working hours.
  • Collaborative and inclusive work environment.
  • Health and wellness programs and initiatives.
